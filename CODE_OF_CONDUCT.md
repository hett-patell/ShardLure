# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1][covenant].

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion
focused on the work. Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory
language are not welcome.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that violates this code, email
hello@networkshard.com with subject `[ShardLure] conduct`.

Reports are handled privately. You'll get an acknowledgement within
72 hours.

[covenant]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
